# ASM Reference

This project is supposed to make available a compilation of specific data in regards to general low-level data, making it accessible for people demanding it, or for newbies to learn with less effort. Also, making people don't need to query with AI — Information in these regards are often hard to find, at least when you want something very specific.

## Contributions

Feel free to write for this project, you may write an article at any time, or a specific topic if missing. 
You may either make a pull request, or send me an email.

## Regarding AI

Unless I don't notice, I will deny any contribution with the use of AI. Assuming AI is bad, the exception "I don't notice" is because if I really do not perceive any AI trait, the quality might be good enough.
